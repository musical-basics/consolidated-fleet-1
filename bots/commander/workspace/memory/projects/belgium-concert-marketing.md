# Belgium Concert Marketing

> High-level strategic source of truth for Lionel Yu's June 11, 2026 Belgium concert marketing push.

## Mission

The near-term goal is to sell at least 100 tickets allocated to Lionel's own channel for the June 11, 2026 concert in Zaventem, Belgium.
Those first 100 tickets are the floor, not the ceiling. If they move, marketing should continue into the remaining hall inventory unless a later decision changes that.

Concert details:
- Event: Lionel Yu — Klassieke Piano en EDM
- Date: Thursday, June 11, 2026
- Time: 19:30 CEST
- Venue: Theaterzaal Maupertuis, CC De Factorij, Zaventem, Belgium

## Strategic importance

This concert matters beyond direct ticket revenue.
Lionel's performance fee is modest relative to the opportunity, and the bigger value is reputational proof plus footage that can support a high-value Normandy castle wedding pitch planned for July 14, 2026.
A full hall and strong live documentation would strengthen that case.

For practical business structure purposes inside the Musical Basics server, Concert Marketing should be treated like the other two business lines.
Its product is an experience rather than a deliverable, but it still functions as a comparable business unit.

## Commercial and relationship constraints

Ticket pricing is fixed by venue policy and should not be changed in marketing strategy.
Current published pricing is €29 standard and €59 VIP, with VIP sold through the organizer side.

The concert is organized by Lions Club Zaventem National Airport Erasmus as a charity fundraiser.
Belgian-facing marketing must not undercut that charity framing, even if Lionel's own channels use a different emphasis.

Luc Ghijselinck is the key organizer contact and should be treated carefully.
He has been patient, has been asking for promotional movement since February, and must be kept informed regularly on sales progress.
Silence from Lionel's side is a relationship risk.

## Current status

As of 2026-04-17, the campaign is behind schedule.
Lionel told Luc on April 1 that full marketing operations would begin very soon, but nothing has shipped yet.
The concert is roughly 55 days out at this point.

## Audience strategy

Primary audience is Lionel's existing audience in Belgium, the Netherlands, Germany, France, and the UK, especially those within practical travel range of Brussels Airport / Zaventem.
Secondary audience is broader EU-based Musical Basics fans who may travel for a rare European appearance.
Local Belgian/Flemish classical and EDM crossover audiences are relevant too, but that is more in Luc's lane than Lionel's internal execution lane.

## Commander-level open decisions

Commander owns the strategic calls on:
- whether the funnel should use a dedicated landing page with email capture or go directly to the venue ticket link
- whether this is truly Lionel's only 2026 European date, which affects urgency-based copy
- whether rehearsal footage involving academy students can be used in promotion without foregrounding the academy / charity-with-kids angle
- whether higher-level press outreach, including prior UK classical press relationships, should be pursued

## Execution split

Commander owns strategy, approvals, memory, and coordination.
Worker bot owns execution planning and should draft the detailed content calendar, sales tracking updates, and draft outreach/supporting materials.
